Chem++

Chem++

Chem++ is the new industry standard when it comes to learning high school chemistry. With our easy to use interface, our users can access everything they need for studying all in one application without needing to open up multiple texts books or browsers. Chem++ will be the future of high school chemistry.

Element Information

Chem++ Offer quick and efficient access to information of all elements. It offeres 17 characteristics including Charge(s), Electron configuration, as well as Melting point.

It does this all in one window which will soon have a copy feature which will copy all the text straight to your clipboard.

Bohr Diagram

Chem++ Offers Bohr Diagrams. The bohr diagrams are generated each and everytime through Markus Frigaard's bohr generation algorithm.

The different levels of electrons i.e. "S", "P", "D", and "F" each recieve their own colours for clarity when using the application.

The amount of electrons and the charge of the element are displayed in the top left corner.

Unit Conversion

Chem++ offers unit converting. Instead of taking out a calculator and periodic table you can simply enter the element which you would like to convert units of and Ta-Da

It can convert units of Grams to Mols to Patricles and right back again, all in a matter of milliseconds!

Search Bar

Chem++ is equipped with a search bar. Said search bar retrieves results from the chemistry stack exchange.

At the moment these results need to be formatted. The other feature to be released in the future will be the option to save these results for later viewing.

Compound Diagrams

Chem++ allows users to retrieve diagrams of chemical compounds in the easiest way imaginable.

Through the process of webscraping Chem++ searches the Pubchem data base of over 33 million compounds for these structures to be returned to you in seconds!
